Quality Control

Two checkpoints before your goods leave China. Independent eyes on every order.

1
First Checkpoint
Mid-Production Inspection
After production reaches about 30%, an inspector visits the workshop. The goal is simple: catch issues before they grow.
  • Materials match the agreed spec
  • Construction follows the approved sample
  • Finish quality at this stage
  • Photos and a short report within 24 hours
2
Second Checkpoint
Pre-Shipment Check
After production is complete — before you pay the balance — a full inspection runs. Nothing leaves the warehouse unchecked.
  • Quantity count against the order
  • Finish, colour, and dimensions checked
  • Packing quality and carton condition
  • Full photo set and video walk-through
Who Checks

Third-Party Inspectors

We use independent QC companies — not factory staff, not our own team. They report to you. We receive the same report at the same time. Nothing is filtered.

Every report reaches you before the final payment is due. Your call on every shipment. No exceptions.
