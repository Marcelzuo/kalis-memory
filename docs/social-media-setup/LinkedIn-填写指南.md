- 鼠标移到头像上 → 出现 **📷 相机图标** → 点击

### ③ 编辑职业简介（最重要！）

这是整个 Profile 的「简介卡片」区域——显示名字下方的铅笔图标 **✏️**。

**点击那个铅笔图标**，会弹出一个编辑窗口，里面包含：

| 字段（中文界面） | 对应英文 | 粘贴内容 |
|-----------------|---------|---------|
| **名字** · **姓氏** | Name | 已经是 Max Zuo，可以保留或改成全称 |
| **职业头衔** | Headline | 见下方 ↓ |
| **所在地** | Location | `Foshan, Guangdong, China` |
| **简介** | About | 见下方 ↓（点「添加简介」展开文本框） |

**职业头衔** 粘贴：

```
Founder & Sourcing Director at KALIS TORIK | Furniture from China | 100+ Vetted Factories | Serving EU Buyers Since 2005
```

**简介** 粘贴（点「添加简介」或「编辑简介」后出现的文本框）：

```
In 2005, I walked into my first furniture factory in Foshan. The noise of the workshop, the smell of fresh lacquer, the rows of unfinished bed frames — it wasn't just a factory. It was a world most European buyers never see.

And that's been the problem.

For 20 years, I've watched overseas buyers overpay for furniture not because the product was expensive — but because someone in the middle added a markup and called it "sourcing." I decided to fix that.

Today I run KALIS TORIK, a Chinese furniture sourcing partner built on one principle: transparency.

We don't pass problems to our clients. We find them first.

——What We Do

• 100+ vetted factories across China (Beds, Sofas, Tables)
• Independent QC at every production stage — we reject what doesn't pass
• CIF quotes delivered within 24 hours
• Full logistics support from factory floor to your warehouse door

——Who We Serve

European furniture retailers, hotel chains, restaurant groups, and interior designers. Small-to-medium businesses who need a sourcing partner that treats their budget like its own.

—— Why It Matters

The difference between a good supplier and a bad one is often invisible until the container arrives. I've seen shipments worth €80,000 get rejected because no one checked them before they left the factory.

That doesn't happen at KALIS TORIK. We audit. You source. No loose ends.

If you're buying furniture from China — or thinking about it — I'm always open for a conversation. No pitch. Just honest answers from someone who's been on the factory floor for two decades.

📩 Reach me here or visit kalistorik.com
```

**所在地**：输入 `Foshan, Guangdong, China`（LinkedIn 会自动匹配下拉选项，选中即可）

编辑完成后点 **「保存」**。
